My Journey into Soy Wax Candles business

Hello My name is Atifa. I am a Textile designer and Quality Control Manager. I am also the proud owner of Scented Designs Soy Candle Company, which I operate out of my home in Mountain House, California. I was always fond of scented candles but my interest grew in scented candles especially soy candles when I did a class project with my son. Everyone liked it so much that I started receiving requests to make it for my family and friends. Later on I have founded my small soy wax candle business in 2021 while studing to become a teacher. This soy candle business of mine quickly grew beyond what I ever imagined from a side hobby to full-time passion, and I'm so grateful to everyone who has supported. My candles are hand-poured into beautiful molds which I custom made or bought from unique places, these are bio-friendly and have no bad effects on health like other parafin wax candles. Soy wax is an excellent fragrance carrier that does not require chemical amplifiers, so these candles are chemical free. I have three lovely kids and live with my loving husband here in the bay area. I take pride in the quality of our products at Ati's, as i do quality checks on batches for sale, and offer the best quality to our customers.
