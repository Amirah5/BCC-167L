Why choose Soy wax candles

Candles can be made out of a variety of different waxes. Each wax has its own unique properties that affect the performance. Candles can be made from paraffin wax, soy wax, palm wax, and granulated wax. Paraffin wax, one of the most popular candle waxes due to its cheap costs, is derived from oil purification process of petroleum, coal, or oil shale. When you remove the waxy substance from crude oil, you’re left with paraffin wax. Whereas Soy wax is a 100% natural wax derived from the vegetable soybeans. Soy beans are harvested,cleaned, cracked, de-hulled and rolled into flakes, soybean oil is extracted from these flakes.Our fragrances are 100% phthalate free and biodegradable, hence reduces synthetic additives which often cause headaches, allergy flare ups and respiratory issues.
